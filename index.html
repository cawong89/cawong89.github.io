I am a mathematician and computational scientist with a focus on numerical analysis and scientific computing for modeling problems in physical sciences and engineering. I currently work at TSMC.

My areas of expertise include applied functional analysis, partial differential equations (PDE), numerical linear algebra, optimization, mathematics of deep learning, and mathematical and computational physics.

I received a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley, advised by John A. Strain, and a B.S. in Applied and Computational Mathematics at the California Institute of Technology.
