One of my primary interests is exploiting functional and harmonic analysis, combined with numerical analysis, in order to construct rigorous numerical approximations that can serve as the foundation for fast and efficient computational methods for physical sciences. To this end, specific topics that I have studied include quadrature rules, structured matrix algorithms, and approximation theory for deep neural networks.
